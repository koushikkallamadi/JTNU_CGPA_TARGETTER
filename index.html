JNTU Grading System Overview

All calculations are based on the official 10-point credit-based grading system followed by JNTUA, JNTUH, and JNTUK, ensuring accurate SGPA, CGPA, and percentage results.

SGPA Formula

SGPA = Σ(Ci × Gi) / ΣCi

Ci = Subject Credits, Gi = Grade Points

CGPA Calculation

CGPA = Σ(Si × Ci) / ΣCi

Si = Semester SGPA, Ci = Semester Credits

Percentage Conversion

(CGPA − 0.5) × 10
